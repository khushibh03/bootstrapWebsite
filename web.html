ABOUT US

Our Story

Since 2002, Ruby’s has brought global cafe culture to Bangalore City, offering casual, locally sourced cuisine, specialty drinks, and a full coffee program. Open for brekkie, lunch, and dinner 7 days a week, Ruby’s iconic menu features our signature bronte burger, taste-driven pasta, bowls and salads, and brunch with cupcakes.
